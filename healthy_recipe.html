Healthy Snack

Tomato and cucumber provide a healthy base for breakfast.

Total time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 5 wulnuts
  • cream cheese
  • 1 slice of whole grain bread

Preparation

  1. Spread the cream cheese on the slice of bread.

    Time: 1 minutes

  2. Top the bread with slice of tomatoes and cucumber.

    Time: 3 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    Time: 1 minute
